Key Features

Card Management

Add and organize credit cards with details like last 4 digits, billing dates, and credit limits.

Payment Tracking

Monitor due amounts, payment dates, and statement balances to never miss a payment.

Secure Sync

Sync data securely with Supabase cloud or store locally with Hive for offline access.

OAuth Login

Sign in effortlessly with Google or GitHub for a seamless and secure experience.
